Frequently Asked Questions

What is LeHS?

LeHS is a platform where you can play coding games that teach you HTML and CSS. By registering a LeHS account, you can access free games, save your progress, and share your achievements.

When is the next game being released?

We’re constantly at work on new game titles. But given the nature of software development, it’s hard to give a firm date until we’re close to release. Follow us on Instagram or Facebook to stay up to date on news.

Can I share my achievements with others?

Yes, by default your profile is public, and displays your in-game progress and achievements.

What are coins?

Coins are the reward given for each correct answer in the Practice module. They can be used to buy hints for the Play part, or you can see some statistics based on them.

What is XP?

XP means experience. You can get the most experience by completing levels in the Play part. You need XP in order to advance with game levels. The general leaderboard is based on XP.
